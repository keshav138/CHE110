OUR SERVICES

Made from wood pulp, paper is highly biodegradable and can decompose relatively quickly under the right conditions. It's widely used for packaging, writing, and various other purposes.

Similar to paper, cardboard is also biodegradable as it's made from wood fibers. It's commonly used for packaging and shipping materials.

Naturally derived from trees, wood is biodegradable and can decompose over time. It's used in construction, furniture making, and various other applications..

Cotton fibers are biodegradable and can break down in compost or soil. They are commonly used in clothing, textiles, and even in some medical applications..
